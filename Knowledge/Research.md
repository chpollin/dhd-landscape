# Research

## Research Questions

1. **Where** is Digital Humanities researched in the DACH region?
2. **What** disciplines and methods are represented, and where?
3. Are there **clusters** of specific DH subdisciplines?
4. How has the DH landscape **evolved** (if temporal data is available)?
5. What **gaps** exist — regions or disciplines underrepresented?

## Context

The Digital Humanities in the German-speaking world have grown significantly since the 2000s. The DHd-Verband (Digital Humanities im deutschsprachigen Raum) is the central community organization. Despite this growth, there is no interactive, filterable overview of who does what where.

## Methodological Note: Promptotyping

This project is simultaneously a research output and a demonstration of the **Promptotyping method**. The iterative cycle of data collection → modeling → visualization → verification is documented transparently in this repository.

Each "Promptotyping-Interface" (web visualization) is a testable hypothesis about the data — it can be verified, corrected, and refined through feedback.
